# Requirements Gathering Quickstart

Use this for small or low-risk requests.

## Minimum Questions

1. What problem are we solving?
2. Who is the user or stakeholder?
3. What should happen when this is complete?
4. What system, record, file, or process is involved?
5. What are the acceptance criteria?
6. What edge cases or blockers do we already know about?
7. Who owns the next step?

## Minimum User Story Format

As a [user/persona], I want [action], so that [value].

## Minimum Acceptance Criteria

- Given [context], when [action], then [expected result].
- The request is complete when [testable condition].
- Known exceptions are documented.

## Output

At the end of lightweight requirements gathering, you should have:

- one user story
- clear acceptance criteria
- known open questions
- owner for next action
- priority or backlog placement
