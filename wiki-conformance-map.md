## Adaptation Notes

The wiki provides the Agile user story lifecycle. The original training document provided practical discovery lessons from a real call. This repo combines them by using the wiki as the main structure and embedding the discovery lessons as supporting practices.

The key additions beyond the wiki are:

- source-of-truth identification
- workflow mapping across systems
- edge case discovery
- audit/logging/visibility requirements
- clear separation of confirmed decisions and open questions
- sprint readiness checks
